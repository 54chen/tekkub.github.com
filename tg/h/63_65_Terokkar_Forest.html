  • Aldorks or Scrybabies?

    You need to pick which faction you will gain rep with. This is a decision YOU MUST MAKE FOR YOURSELF! Pop open your web browser, go to wowwiki.com, click on "Aldor v Scryer" on the left, and look at the rewards for each faction. Make sure you check the quest rewards and crafting recipes as well. Select the appropriate quest from Khadgar once you've decided.

  • Collection Quests...

    While you're running around, keep an eye out for Olemba Cones (bright blue things on the ground). Also kill Warp Stalkers and Timber Worgs as you run across them. And lastly, save any Arakkoa Feathers you get, you'll need them for a quest later for Lower City rep.

    The Blackwood Corrupted

    Blackwood camps are east of Bashal'Aran. Gather grain (50.65, 34.99), nuts (51.83, 33.56) and fruit (52.87, 33.38). Use the cleansing bowl near the bonfire. Kill Xabraxxis when he spawns.

  • Tharnariun's Hope

    To south between the two camps, up the hill is a cave (51.55, 37.50). Kill the adds, run away and reset the Den Mother. Return and kill her.
